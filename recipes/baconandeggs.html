Howl's Breakfast

Description

Wake up to a delicious breakfast of bacon, eggs, a slice of bread, and a slice of cheese. Guaranteed to keep you full for the morning since it is packed with protein and carbs!

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of thick cut bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 loaf of bread
  • 1 slab of cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat since we do not have the talking fire from Howl's Moving Castle.
  2. Add in the 3 slices of bacon once the pan is hot enough. Cook until crispy and push to one side of pan.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium and crack in the 4 eggs. Aiming for sunny side up, but cook them to your liking.
  4. Remove the pan from the heat and set aside.
  5. Cut a off desired slice of bread from loaf and plate all the ingredients. Serve and enjoy!
  6. optional: Toast the bread and lay the room temperature cheese over the hot toast.
