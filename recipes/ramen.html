Ponyo's Ramen

Description

Simple and easy to make. Quickly whisk up this delicious and fulfilling bowl of tasty ramen for a quick meal. Will leave you feeling like you are in a hot spring after consumption!

Ingredients

  • 1 instant ramen noodle packet of your liking
  • 1 egg hard or soft boiled
  • 1 green onion chopped
  • 1 piece of ham cut in half
  • 2.5 cups of water

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients. Cut the ham and egg in half, chop the green onions thinly.
  2. Boil water in a kettle or in a pot on the stove.
  3. Place ramen into a bowl. If it comes with a seasoning packet, make sure to open and sprinkle on the dry noodles now.
  4. Once water is boiled, take it off the stove and pour over the noodles in the bowl. Make sure to place a lid or plate over the noodles and let sit for 3 minutes.
  5. When the noodles are soft to your liking and you are ready to start eating, place the ham and egg on top of the ramen and sprinkle with chopped green onion then enjoy!
